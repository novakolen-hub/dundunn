3. CAPITAL DEPLOYMENT GUARDRAILS (Phase 2 spec)
   - Swing exposure cap: market value / equity per GP
     A=40%, B=30%, C-R=15%, D=20%
   - Breakeven stop discount: positions with stop ≥ entry count at 50%
   - Intraday risk budget: sum of DT dollar risk / equity
     A=2.5%, B=1.5%, C-R=0.5%, D=1.5%
   - Max concurrent DTs: A=4, B=3, C-R=1, D=2
   - Scorecard Check 9: Portfolio Heat
   - Existing positions grandfathered on GP transitions

KEY DECISIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. SIZING: % of equity, not fixed dollars. Current live equity reference.
2. DEPLOYED CAPITAL: Hybrid — size per total equity, cap total deployment.
   Swings at market value, DTs at dollar risk.
3. BE DISCOUNT: Yes — swings with stops at breakeven count at 50%.
4. LOSS LIMITS: Advisory, not hard block.
5. SYSTEM DOCS: Live on GitHub Pages, not Google Docs.
   Claude can read + update via API. Single source of truth.
6. GP TRANSITIONS: Grandfather existing positions, only block new entries.
7. PAPER TRADES: Exempt from deployment caps.
8. UNPLANNED TRADES: Tag via toast, track adherence, no hard block.
9. DT→SW: Formal conversion with full carryover + new snapshot.
10. PORTFOLIO VIEW: Theme-based clusters as default, not just SW/Cash split.
